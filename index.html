Sinopsis

The series is a comedy that deals with the adventures and misadventures of a group of 6 friends (Ross, Joey, Chandler, Phoebe, Rachel and Monica) who live in Manhattan, New York, the viewer being a direct witness of the evolution in their lives , with its bad moments too, but as we have already said before, all in a comedy key. In fact, the series has really few serious moments, but the ones it does have are solved with some joke at the end that ends up taking away the iron from the confrontations that may arise between the protagonists. From the first season we already realize that on which couple (Ross and Rachel) the weight of the argument will fall as far as romance is concerned, however (and this I do not put it for you not to gut it) there is a couple with whom nobody counted that it will eat much of the important events in the series. The action takes place between Monica's apartments (the largest) and Chandler's, as well as in a cafeteria called Central Perk. Love affairs are the order of the day (flirting has never been so easy) not only between secondary characters, but also between the protagonists themselves, as is the aforementioned case of Ross and Rachel. 90% of the chapters are self-concluding except for the last of each season that give rise to the next.
